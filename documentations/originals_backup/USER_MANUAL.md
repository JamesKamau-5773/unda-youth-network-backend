## ğŸ” Login & Account Security

### How to Log In

1. Go to the UNDA website
2. Enter your **email** or **username**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **"Login"**

### Password Requirements

Your password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contain at least one UPPERCASE letter
- Contain at least one lowercase letter
- Contain at least one number (0-9)
- Contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

**Example of a strong password:** `Unda2026!Safe`

### Account Lockout Protection

For your security, your account will be **locked for 30 minutes** after **7 failed login attempts**. This prevents unauthorized access to your account.

If locked out:
1. Wait 30 minutes
2. Try logging in again
3. If you've forgotten your password, contact your supervisor or admin

### Forgot Your Password?

**Currently:** Contact your supervisor or administrator to reset your password.

**Coming Soon:** Self-service password reset via email.

### Security Tips

âœ… **DO:**
- Keep your password private
- Use a unique password (don't reuse passwords from other sites)
- Log out when using shared computers
- Contact admin if you notice suspicious activity

âŒ **DON'T:**
- Share your password with anyone
- Write your password down where others can see it
- Use simple passwords like "password123"
- Leave your account logged in on public computers

3. Answer each question honestly
4. Submit the assessment
5. View your results and personalized recommendations

**Your results are:**
- âœ… Confidential (only you and authorized supervisors can see them)

**Severity Levels Explained:**
